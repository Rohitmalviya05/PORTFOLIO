COMMUNIICATION & BOOK CLUB


COURSE OVERVIEW
--Book club and Social Emotional

This course encourages a love for reading through exploration of diverse books. Students discuss stories, characters, and themes, enhancing reading comprehension and critical thinking. Through group discussions, they gain new perspectives and deeper insights into literature.


--COMMUNICATION

This course focuses on building emotional intelligence and social skills. Students engage in activities that promote empathy, self-awareness, and communication. By learning to manage emotions and build positive relationships, they develop skills for personal growth and meaningful connections. Grasp and implement the foundational principles of communication within their interactions. Think freely, recall the topics taught in class and implement them. Engage in conversations using grammatically accurate English, while also incorporating nuanced phonetics, vocabulary, intonation, and improved pronunciation which is free of MTI.Public speaking skills.
